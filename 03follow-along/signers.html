Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Indepence include

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress and famous for his large, bold signature on the Declaration of Independence, which became a symbol of defiance against British rule.
Thomas Jefferson
The primary author of the Declaration of Independence and later served as the third President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned inventor, scientist, and diplomat who helped secure French support for the American colonies during the Revolutionary War.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence who later became the second President of the United States and played a key role in diplomacy.
Samuel Adams
A revolutionary leader and organizer, known for his role in the Boston Tea Party and early resistance to British authority.
Roger Sherman
The only Founding Father to sign all four major founding documents, including the Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States, and playes a key role in drafting foundational documents and promoting the cause of freedom.
