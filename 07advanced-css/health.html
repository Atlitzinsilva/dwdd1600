Health

Preventive Care

Preventive care helps your cat stay healthy and can reduce emergencies. Cats often hide discomfort, so routine care matters even when your cat seems fine.

Nutrition and Hydration

A balanced diet supports energy, skin, coat, and immunity. Provide fresh water daily and feed appropriate portions for your cat's age and activity level.

Warning Signs

If you notice sudden changes in appetite, energy, or litter box habits, contact a veterinarian. This information is educational and not a medical diagnosis.

For emergencies, call your veterinarian.
